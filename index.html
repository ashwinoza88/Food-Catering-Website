Food Items

Handvo

Handvo is a popular Gujarati dish that boasts a unique blend of flavors and textures. This savory cake is made from a fermented batter of rice, lentils, and vegetables, spiced with a medley of aromatic herbs and spices. With its crispy exterior and soft, spongy interior, handvo is a delightful culinary experience that is both satisfying and comforting.

Khaman

Khaman, a traditional Gujarati delicacy, is a fluffy and savory steamed cake made from gram flour (besan). This light and spongy dish is seasoned with mustard seeds, curry leaves, and a tangy touch of lemon juice, creating a perfect balance of flavors. Served with a drizzle of chutney or a sprinkle of sev, khaman is a popular snack or breakfast option that never fails to tantalize the taste buds.

Jalebi-Fafda

Jalebi and Fafda is a classic and beloved combination from the state of Gujarat in India. Crispy, golden Fafda paired with deep-fried swirls of sweet Jalebi creates a perfect balance of flavors and textures. This indulgent duo is a popular choice for breakfast or as a festive treat, offering a delightful blend of sweet and savory in every bite.
